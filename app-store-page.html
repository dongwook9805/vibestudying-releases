Privacy Policy

VibeStudying does not require account registration to use the macOS app. Most study data such as documents, notes, drawings, and flashcards is stored locally on the user's device.

The app may handle the following information locally on your device:

  • Imported PDF files and file names
  • Highlights, notes, lecture notes, and drawings
  • Flashcards and image occlusion study data
  • Audio recordings created by you inside the app
  • Optional API key entered by you for AI features

If you choose to use AI features, text, images, or study content selected by you may be sent to third-party AI providers to generate explanations, OCR suggestions, flashcards, or analysis results. These AI features are optional.

We do not sell your personal data. Data is shared with third-party AI services only when you explicitly use remote AI-powered features.

Microphone access is requested only if you use recording or speech features. File access is requested only when you choose files to import, save, or export.

For privacy-related questions, contact support@vibestudying.app.
